Press ` (the unshifted ~ key) during game play to display the console window, then enter one of the following codes to activate the corresponding cheat function.

All completion bonuses:
Note: This procedure involves editing a game file; create a backup copy of the file before proceeding. Use a text editor to edit the .cfg file that corresponds to your player in the "base" folder in the game folder. Find the "seta g_secretCount" line and change its value so it appears as:
seta g_secretCount "70"

This sets the number of Golden Starships found to 70, which unlocks all completion bonuses.

Completion bonuses:
Successfully complete the game to unlock the "Secrets" and "Credits" option at the main menu. Depending on the number of Golden Starships collected during game play, additional bonus levels and features will be unlocked at the "Secrets" screen.

10 Golden Starships: Holodeck - Swamp Cabin
20 Golden Starships: Holodeck - Shuttle Shootdown
35 Golden Starships: Shooter with Korban
45 Golden Starships: Concept Art
60 Golden Starships: Holodeck - Alien Cloning Facility
70 Golden Starships: Character Gallery

Hint: Mission 4: Secret mini-game:
There is a secret mini-game that has Starship items. The entrance counts for a secret credit. Go to where you pick up the "Attrexian Arc Launcher". Shoot the wall at the end of the room (faced when you first enter the vault). A Super Mario Brothers pipe will appear. If you are having problems finding it, use the structural integrity view on your Tricorder. The controls are still the same, but it can be a bit confusing. If you fall off a ledge, you will exit the mini-game, but not die. You can re-enter the pipe at any time.
Information in this section was contributed by Defender.

Hint: Mission 5: Staff weapon:
In the Drull Ruins mission, when you exit the "gas" room (not through where you first entered the room), on the right side of the wall is a series of engraved panels. Shoot them from top to bottom and make a note of the pictures. Next, go back to the beginning, when the ruins tried to kill you with the turrets. There is another open door with tiles on the floor and some sort of light post zapping a vault on the floor. This is where the sketch is used. Step on only the same tiles in the same order as before. The tiles will, in the correct sequence, open the vault and give you access to the staff weapon. Its primary fire launches an energy bolt and secondary fire produces a small shockwave that stuns anyone except for you. [Screenshot]
Information in this section was contributed by Defender.
