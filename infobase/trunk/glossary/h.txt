<dt><a name="hallofmirrors">    </a>Hall-Of-Mirrors     <dd>[HOM] (-effect). The <i>Hall-Of-Mirrors</i> effect is something you get when parts of the screen in the <a href="#fps">FPS</a>-game are not updated due to a <a href="#leak">leak</a> or when your player is outside the level in the <a href="#void">void</a>. The effect was introduced with the DOOM game, where it would look like a long corridor, going into the screen, flickering. It looked like a Hall of Mirrors, hence the name.
